## 一、老师出题风格速写（5 条铁律）

1. **每题 5 个选项**（A-E），单选，1 分/题
2. **题干直问：** "What is X?" / "Which of the following is/is NOT..." / "What does X refer to?" / "Which principle..."
3. **干扰项设计：** 1 个正确 + 3 个明显错误 + 1 个稍微像的
4. **不考：** 年份、罚款百分比、法规缩写（HIPAA/PIPL 等）、具体案例名称（COMPAS/Amazon 等）、3 层对比、tradeoff 细节
5. **只考：** 核心定义（"What is X?"）、主要原则（6 F-P-T-A-S-B）、基本分类（3 框架 / 几大类型）、关键技术（Differential Privacy / XAI）

## 五、考前 30 分钟 · 终极清单

### 🔥 必读 5 分钟版（就是这 20 条）

1. **AI Ethics** = Exploring **moral implications** of AI development and deployment
2. **Utilitarian** = Maximizing overall **well-being or happiness**（Bentham/Mill）
3. **Deontological** = **Duties and moral rules**（Kant）
4. **Virtue ethics** = Cultivating **moral character**（Aristotle / eudaimonia / phronesis）
5. **AI bias** = **Systematic errors or unfairness** in AI systems
6. **Protected attribute** = **Race**（+ gender/age/disability）
7. **Selection bias** = **Training data misrepresents target population**
8. **Temporal bias** = Training data **outdated**
9. **Algorithmic fairness goal** = **Equitable outcomes across demographic groups**
10. **Data privacy** = **Granting individuals control over their personal information**
11. **Differential privacy** = **Adds noise**
12. **Data minimization** = Collect **only necessary** personal data
13. **Transparency** = Making decision processes **understandable**
14. **Explainability** = **Clear explanations for AI outputs**
15. **Right to Explanation** = Transparency to **individuals affected by AI**
16. **Accountability** = **Human oversight and ability to intervene**
17. **Proportionality principle** = 归责原则
18. **Autonomous system** = Performing tasks **without human intervention**
19. **AV dilemma** = Saving passengers vs. minimizing total **casualties**
20. **AWS challenge** = Remove **human moral agency** from warfare

### 🔥 易错干扰项自检

- 见到 "Increased job opportunities" / "Unchanged job market" / "Environmental sustainability"→ **在 AI 负面影响题中是错误选项**
