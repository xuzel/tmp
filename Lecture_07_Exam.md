## ① ⚡ 30 秒速记卡（Core Terms）

- **Social Impact 三维** = **Economic + Informational + Governance**
- **Displacement effect（替代效应）** = AI 替代人类工作 → 失业
- **Complementarity effect（互补效应）** = AI 增强人类能力 → 新岗位
- **3 种独特人类技能**（AI 替代不了）= **Emotional Intelligence + Creativity + Complex Problem-Solving**
- **Skill-Biased Technological Change** = 技能偏向型技术变革（高技能受益/低技能受损）
- **Price discrimination（价格歧视）** = 根据消费画像定不同价格
- **AI billionaires** = AI 财富急速集中的新现象
- **Digital divide（数字鸿沟）** = 从"能否上网"升级为"能否用 AI"
- **Distributive justice（分配正义）** = Rawls 政治哲学概念
- **AI for social good** = AI 向善倡议
- **NLP algorithms** = 生成类人文本（操纵工具之一）
- **Deep learning models** = 用户画像实现定向操纵
- **Sentiment analysis tools** = 衡量公众舆论与情绪
- **Deepfakes** = 生成/篡改视觉内容误导
- **Botnet technology** = 大规模自动化账号协调
- **Recommendation algorithms** = 精细化个性化推送 → 回音壁
- **Psychographic targeting（心理画像定向）** = Cambridge Analytica 核心
- **Echo chambers / Filter bubbles** = 回音壁 / 过滤气泡
- **Misinformation** = 虚假信息
- **Polarization** = 极化
- **Fragmentation** = 碎片化
- **Liar's Dividend（说谎者红利）** = 假内容泛滥让真内容被怀疑
- **Prime Vulnerability Moments** = 脆弱时刻剥削（深夜/失恋/压力大推送广告）
- **Helicopter Research** = 研究者"空降"发展中国家取数据 → 数据殖民主义
- **Data colonialism（数据殖民主义）** = 全球南方数据外流
- **Gig economy platforms** = 零工经济（灵活但不稳定）
- **Dual-use technology** = 双用技术（AI 4 大武器都属此类）

## ③ ⚠️ 易混概念对比表

### A. Displacement vs. Complementarity
| | Displacement | Complementarity |
|---|---|---|
| 方向 | 替代人 | 增强人 |
| 结果 | 失业 | 新岗位 |
| 政策 | 缓冲 | 放大 |
| 共存 | 两种效应同时存在 |

### B. Misinformation vs. Liar's Dividend
| | Misinformation（虚假信息） | Liar's Dividend（说谎者红利） |
|---|---|---|
| 指 | 假的被当真 | **真的被当假** |
| 机制 | 传播 + 影响 | 存在假内容 → 真内容可被否认 |
| 危害 | 误导 | **侵蚀信任根基** |

### C. Echo Chambers vs. Filter Bubbles vs. Fragmentation
- **Echo chamber**：同质声音互相加强
- **Filter bubble**：算法过滤导致接触信息变窄
- **Fragmentation**：不同用户活在不同"信息现实"

### D. Prime Vulnerability Moments vs. Helicopter Research
- **PVM**：剥削 **个体心理弱点**（深夜广告、失恋营销）
- **HR**：剥削 **地理/经济弱者**（发展中国家取数据）

### E. 4 大社交媒体武器 vs. 3 大工业化手段
- **武器**（生成能力）：NLP / DL / Sentiment / Deepfakes
- **手段**（放大机制）：Botnets / Recommendation / Targeting

### F. AI for Social Good 的双刃原则
- **Beneficence**（有益） ↔ **Non-maleficence**（不伤害）
- **Distributive Justice**（公平分配） ↔ **Autonomy**（不操纵）

### G. Gig Economy 双面
- 优势：灵活
- 劣势：**收入不稳 + 福利缺失**

## ⑥ 🕳️ 常见出题陷阱

1. **Displacement 和 Complementarity 不是互斥的**——它们共存，政策任务是最大化后者、缓冲前者。
2. **"独特人类技能" 3 项：EI / Creativity / Complex Problem-Solving**——别漏或替换。
3. **Price discrimination 是"不同人不同价"**，不是"差别对待"的泛指。
4. **Liar's Dividend ≠ Misinformation**：前者是"真被怀疑"，后者是"假被相信"。
5. **Echo chamber / Filter bubble / Fragmentation 三者不同**：同质声音 / 信息窄化 / 现实分裂。
6. **Helicopter Research 指的是"外部研究者空降取数据"**，不是"无人机研究"。
7. **Prime Vulnerability Moments 是"脆弱时刻剥削"**，不是"价格错误"。
8. **4 大武器库**（NLP/DL/Sentiment/Deepfakes） vs. **3 大手段**（Botnets/Recommendation/Targeting）——考题易混分类。
9. **AI billionaires 财富增速史无前例**——不是渐进现象。
10. **Digital divide 新含义 = 能否用 AI**，不只是上网。
11. **Distributive Justice 与 Rawls 相关**——不是经济效率概念。
12. **"AI for Social Good" 也可能带来 4 大非预期后果**（偏见/技能侵蚀/鸿沟/经济扰乱）。
13. **Botnet 是"网络机器人军团"**，不是单一账号。
14. **Psychographic targeting 是 Cambridge Analytica 核心**——这个关键词可能出现。

## ⑦ 🎯 本讲练习题（10 道 MCQ）

**Q1.** Which pair of effects most accurately captures AI's impact on jobs?
- A. Offshoring vs. Reshoring
- B. Displacement vs. Complementarity
- C. Fiscal vs. Monetary
- D. Inflation vs. Deflation

**Q2.** Which of the following is NOT listed as one of the three **uniquely human skills** AI struggles to replace?
- A. Emotional intelligence
- B. Creativity
- C. Complex problem-solving
- D. Speed of calculation

**Q3.** **Price discrimination** in the AI era refers to:
- A. Discrimination against minorities in hiring
- B. Charging different consumers different prices based on AI-inferred profiles
- C. Using AI to set minimum wage
- D. Algorithmic bias in credit scoring

**Q4.** The **Liar's Dividend** describes:
- A. The profit earned from spreading fake news
- B. How widespread fake content makes genuine information easier to dismiss as fake
- C. A loophole in defamation law
- D. A bug in deepfake detectors

**Q5.** Which of the following is NOT one of the four **AI techniques for social media manipulation**?
- A. NLP algorithms
- B. Deep learning models
- C. Sentiment analysis tools
- D. Blockchain consensus protocols

**Q6.** **Helicopter research** in AI ethics refers to:
- A. Using drones for research
- B. Western researchers extracting data from developing regions without meaningful local benefit
- C. Rapid iterative prototyping
- D. Cloud-based federated learning

**Q7.** **Prime Vulnerability Moments** describe:
- A. Optimal windows for patching security bugs
- B. Points when users are emotionally vulnerable and targeted with sales/ads
- C. Times when AI systems are most likely to crash
- D. Peak hours of internet traffic

**Q8.** Which is the best example of **Complementarity effect**?
- A. A chatbot replacing an entire call center
- B. An AI coding assistant enabling developers to produce more high-quality software
- C. An AI system performing legal document review in full automation
- D. An AI layoff announcement

**Q9.** Which concept most directly challenges the traditional distribution of wealth from AI?
- A. Emotional intelligence
- B. Distributive justice
- C. Digital literacy
- D. Gig economy

**Q10.** Which of the following contributes to **Fragmentation** of public discourse?
- A. Equal exposure to all viewpoints
- B. AI-personalized content creating separate information realities
- C. Universal fact-checking
- D. Strict content moderation
